Mission Statement

Spotifoo puts music back in the hands of the user. Our crowsourced API collects and aggregates music artists, songs, and albums. The API also allows for the curation of playlists. Once you're signed up you can construct playlists with the available music and follow other users and their playlists. Through crowdsourcing information, we at Spotifoo strive to build a database where all those with any musical inclination can share music that makes them want to belt out as if they were alone atop a mountain.
